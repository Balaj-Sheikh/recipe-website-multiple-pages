Tips

  • To enhance the taste and flavour of this dish, dry roast the whole spices, grind them and add it to the gravy.
  • To make the dish rich and creamy, you can add some fresh cream. This will not only accentuate the taste but at the same time it will make it creamy in texture and mellow down the ...
  • You can also use coconut cream or coconut milk in the place of fresh cream.
  • You can also precook your mutton koftas and then add it to the gravy to save time.
